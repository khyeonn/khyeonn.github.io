Hi, I’m Max. I am a mechanical engineer with a strong interest in **robotics, autonomous systems, and control theory**, with experience spanning control systems, mechatronics, and embedded software.

### Focus Areas
- Robotics and autonomous systems  
- Control systems and state estimation  
- Physics-based simulation and system modeling  
- Embedded systems and real-time software  


### Education
I earned my B.S. and M.S. in Mechanical Engineering from the **University of Maryland** in 2023 and 2024. During my studies, I worked on projects that integrated mechanical, electrical, and software components to address complex engineering problems. Much of my work focused on **system modeling, state estimation, and control**, with an emphasis on translating theory into practical implementations.

### Professional Work
Professionally, I work as a **Control Engineer** developing industrial automation and control software for live, safety-critical systems. In these environments, control correctness, deterministic behavior, and system uptime are non-negotiable. This experience has reinforced disciplined development practices, rigorous testing methodologies, and system-level debugging under real operational constraints.

## Hobbies
Outside of work, I spend much of my time building projects to deepen my understanding of robotics and autonomous systems. 

When I’m not debugging code or fixing broken hardware, I enjoy cooking and exploring new restaurants around the city. I’m a big foodie and love experimenting with new recipes and discovering hidden gems.

If you're interested in what I'm currently working on, you can follow along on my [blog](/blog/)!
